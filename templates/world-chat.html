Community Guidelines

Please follow these rules to keep our community safe and professional.

Be Respectful: Treat everyone with kindness. Harassment or hate speech will not be tolerated.
Stay Professional: This is a career-focused platform. Keep discussions relevant to skills and growth.
No Spamming: Do not flood the chat or promote unrelated services/links.
